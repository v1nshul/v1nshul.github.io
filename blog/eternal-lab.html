Eternal Labyrinth: A Journey Through Reality and Illusion

A short story about a shifting maze, simulation, and perception.

A protagonist wakes into a misty, shifting maze after a strange disturbance at home. Paths appear and close without warning, a shadow follows, and a mysterious old man suggests that the world itself may be trapped in a system of control.

The story moves between fear, escape, and uncertainty before returning to the question beneath it all: whether reality is only what we perceive, or whether perception itself can be manipulated.
