The 2024-25 job market experience

Humbling.

The 2024-25 job market was a humbling experience. After finishing university submissions, the process became a waiting game: applications, online assessments, video interviews, rejections, and very few final-stage opportunities.

The mentality needed to survive it is to stay optimistic and keep going. The numbers became difficult to ignore: hundreds of applications, many assessments, and only a small number of face-to-face interviews.

It is the kind of experience that makes people doubt themselves, even when they are still putting in the work. The post captures that uncertainty honestly while continuing to look for the right opportunity.
