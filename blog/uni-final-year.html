The final year university experience

Reflection on how my final year went.

In my opinion, final year is the year where one learns the most. It gives you a reason to look back, reflect, and understand how you are reaching the end of an academic phase.

My final year was the best university year for me personally. I knew exactly what to give my time and energy to. I was cooking more, dealing with the job market, meeting new people, making friends, and trying my best.

In terms of learning, final year gave me freedom with my project. I read research papers, built a larger virtual library than before, and worked on something I genuinely wanted to understand.

Alongside studying, I learned new skills, improved my cooking, and found that there is learning every day if you look in the right direction. I will remember the year as something I am thankful for.
