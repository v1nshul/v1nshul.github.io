Moderation and Censorship in Media

Effective social media moderation balances free speech and safety.

Social media gives people a platform to share thoughts and emotions, but it also creates difficult questions about abuse, threats, misinformation, and who should be responsible for moderation.

The post explores the role of technology companies, oversight boards, moderators, bots, and AI moderation systems, while arguing that moderation should protect safety without casually removing the ability to speak freely online.

It concludes that moderation should be handled through transparent rules and groups of decision-makers rather than a single person deciding what is right or wrong for everyone.
